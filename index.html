I have been doing research on several aspects of static/program analysis for validation and verification of safety-critical systems through static analysis and automatic test data generation. More particularly, I am aiming at improving precision and performance of static analysis tools while maintaining their scalability on very large software systems, identification and implementation of verification techniques to detect of new types of defects, improving and supporting manual inspection of the results of static analysis tools, etc. My current research includes (a) improving performance when the model checking is used to eliminate false positives from static analysis warnings, and (b) supporting users in their manual reviewing of static analysis warnings through review assistance framework. My earlier research includes variability management in Software Product Lines Engineering, effectiveness of various coverage criteria (MCDC, MBVC), designing new verification properties, etc.
