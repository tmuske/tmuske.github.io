Work summary:

I work as a scientist at TRDDC, one of the research-and-innovation labs of Tata Consultancy Services Ltd, located in Pune, India.

My research interests span across several aspects of program analysis, software verification, and software engineering.
Also, currently I am exploring ways to use machine larning to advance the state of postprocessing of alarms.
More details about my current and prior work can be found here.

Before joining TRDDC, TCS, in 2007, I worked with IBM India as a developer for around 15 months.
